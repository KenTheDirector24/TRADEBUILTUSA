Impact Drivers

How impact drivers work, where they earn their keep on an HVAC/R job, and how to pick the right bits and settings.

What It Is

An impact driver is a compact, high-torque power tool built to drive screws and small fasteners fast, without stalling out or twisting out of your hand. It looks like a drill's smaller cousin, but the two tools solve different problems — a drill spins smoothly for boring holes and driving light fasteners; an impact driver adds concussive rotational force so it can drive long screws into dense material without you having to lean into it.

On an HVAC/R crew it's one of the most-used tools in the bag, right alongside a drill/driver and a set of nut drivers.
